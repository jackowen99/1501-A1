The above are basic 7x7 and 8x8 boards. These will require a lot of backtracking and both programs will take a long time to run. However they may not be as bad as you would initially think. One thing to keep in mind is that because the words must all be longer, there may in fact be fewer possible solutions than there would be for the smaller board sizes. In fact neither of these boards has a solution. Note: If you know that you will have a full-sized board of 7 or 8, how could you optimize your algorithm to make it faster?
